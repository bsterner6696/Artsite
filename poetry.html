My Poetry

I really enjoy writing poetry. It serves as a healthy way to vent emotions and deal with the issues bothering me. When I write poetry I prefer to write in free verse. I feel that poetry is more honest when it is not constrained by the rules of other formats. Although, I believe that certain forms of poetry, such as haiku, can be excellent tools for improving as a writer. Poetry works best when it can force as much impact into as few words as possible. A form that requires a very small amount of syllables is excellent practice for honing this skill. I also keep a tumblr blog which I write on.
