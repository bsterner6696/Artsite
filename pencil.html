Pencil and Charcoal Drawing


My favorite medium is pencil or charcoal. I enjoy drawing with pencil or charcoal because they're quick and forgiving, mistakes-wise. I almost always have a sketchbook with me as well as a collection of pencils. My favorite subjects to draw are landscapes and portraits. I find great beauty in nature, and to capture that beauty on paper is an aspiration of mine.
